The Laplace Transform: $\ \mathcal{L}\{f(t)\} = \int\limits_{0}^{\infty}f(t)\,e^{-st}dt = \tilde{f}(s)$

$f(t)$ $\mathcal{L}\{f(t)\} = \tilde{f}(s)$
$1$ $\frac{1}{s}$
$e^{at}$ $\frac{1}{s-a}$
$\sin(at)$ $\frac{a}{s^2\,+\,a^2}$
$\cos(at)$ $\frac{s}{s^2\,+\,a^2}$
$\sqrt{t}$ $\frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2\sqrt{s}}$
